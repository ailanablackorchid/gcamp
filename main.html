It’s time to make own contribution for the sustainable future of the Planet

The mission of the Green Campus initiative is to protect the environment and ensure sustainability in all aspects of campus life - operations, research, teaching, construction and outreach.

The vision of the Green Campus initiative is to become a university with a sustainable culture in which all students and employees practice sustainable behavior in everyday life.

About Green Campus initiative

Sustainable development teaches that development should be pursued in a manner whereby the needs of present generation will not compromise the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. And it has become a global call for action.

Universities play a key role in paving the path to a sustainable future as they educate future leaders of our society. To this end, many universities have developed programs to foster applied research and education by using their campuses to test real-time sustainability solutions.
Nazarbayev University through the Green Campus initiative intends to advance sustainability in operations across the campus and to develop an environmentally conscious culture throughout and beyond the University.

Our main task is to create an environment for students, faculty and staff to have first- hand experience about what it means to ‘go green’. NU strives to a sustainable future through education, research, innovation and campus infrastructure.
