What sustainability means for us?

General Sustainability concept is based on three pillars: environmental, economic and social.

Environmental aspect represents natural resource use, environmental management and pollution prevention (Campus performance).

Social aspect reflects cultural aspect as education, community, and social involvement (Culture).

Economic aspect includes profit and cost saving (Financial viability).

All three elements should be reflected in our future sustainability efforts.
